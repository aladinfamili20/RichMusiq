Rich Musiq Dennis, popularly known by his stage name Rich Musiq , is a Tanzanian bongo flava recording artist, dancer and a businessman from Kasulu, Kigoma. He has had several hit songs including "Nitarejeah" which he featured Congo artist Bro James.
